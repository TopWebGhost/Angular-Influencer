Features for PR & Agencies

Search Engine

The majority of bloggers target a style-niche based on their own tastes. We provide analytical data which helps you uncover the influencers where your products/brands will resonate the most with their audience.

Real-Time Profiles

Ever been a victim of zeroing in on the perfect influencer, only to notice that their last post was 8 months ago? Never again! All profiles, links, social counts, and activity levels are updated in real-time.

Unlimited Users

Have a whole team who wants to access The Shelf? Great! The more the merrier, with multiple logins at no additional cost per person you can have a whole PR army closing in on the right influencers at Olympic speed.

Download Custom Lists

You requested it, we listened! As a result, we’re launching this feature as part of the PR Plan. Organize your collections and download them as a spreadsheet for easy reference.

Outreach Tools

Our series of outreach tools act like a CRM system, allowing you to send out all of your emails, invite influencers to campaigns, track opens, and more, all in one place!

Relvancy is Key

When it comes to influencer marketing, the phrase quality over quantity reigns true! Finding influencers who are referencing relevant topics and products, as well as who target the right demographic is actually way more crucial and effective than finding just anyone with a ton of traffic.
