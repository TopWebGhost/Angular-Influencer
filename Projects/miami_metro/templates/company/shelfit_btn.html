First Install Your Bookmarklet

A bookmarklet is a tool that you can use to "Shelve" items that you like while shopping. They are easy to use, and if you've ever used one before (like on Pinterest, or something...) then you'll know what to do. Just drag the bookmarklet up to your bookmarks bar.
If you haven't used a bookmarklet before, you can watch the video below to see how to do it on your browser.
