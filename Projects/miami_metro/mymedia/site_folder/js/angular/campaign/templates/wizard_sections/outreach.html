When you’re doing influencer outreach, templates are so much easier! That’s why we’ve created six that you can use for each step of the way. Review and edit them here as they will become your default templates. Once you’re ready for outreach, simply hit “Send” and the associated template will get sent out. If you want to edit the template to a particular influencer during outreach, you can do that right from the pipeline.
Please be careful about the "merge tags". Merge tags are the words contained in double brackets. These function like variables and will load in things like the influencer's name, their blog name, links to their contract and campaign overview. All of these merge tags fill in data that is specific to the influencer. They can be moved. But if they are edited, they will no longer work. (Please ask your account manager to review anything you’re unsure of.)
Initial Outreach
This template will be the first email sent to influencers asking them if they would be interested in collaborating. The template to the right is very generic and we encourage you to rewrite it with more specific details.

If you’re new to influencer marketing, your account manager can send you a suggested template or review yours before you start sending it out. Initial impressions are everything!
Follow Up Emails
The majority of influencers don’t respond until your second email. We’ve made it easy to keep track of these people by adding them to the Follow Up stage of your pipeline. From there you can use this template as a gentle reminder to see if they’re interested in your campaign.
Details + Contracts
Once you’ve worked out the logistics of your campaign, it’s time to collect details from the influencer. This template should work for most campaigns but please review and make edits as need be.

The details you collect from the influencers are determined by your settings below, but in most cases, the details collected will feed into your contracts, the influencer’s overview page, and their product selections.
Shipping
If you’re sending out packages to influencers, you’ll want to know they’ve received them and are on their way to creating content. By adding the shipping tracking code in your influencer pipeline, you can then send out this email in case they need to track their packages. Plus, once they click on that link, you’ll be notified that they’ve received their packages. Done and done!

Reminder + Tracking Links
Before your campaign goes live, you can send your influencers a reminder. This email should encourage them to review their campaign overview page and requirements one last time.

Additionally, it provides them with a link to their tracking codes, which they’ll need to add to their blog post (if you enable this feature). Tracking codes allow us to track blog engagement, impressions, and click-throughs. If you have any questions about tracking codes, please contact your account manager.
Payment Complete
This email will simply allow you to notify the influencers that you’ve paid them for the campaign.
